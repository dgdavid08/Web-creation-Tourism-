Sierra Hotel

Directions

Set on a leafy street among low-rise houses and restaurants, this simple hotel is within 2 km of Sibulan Airport, Silliman Beach and the ferry terminal. Modern, unfussy rooms offer flat-screen TVs, desks, sitting areas, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Some feature balconies and/or minifridges. Suites add dining and sitting areas, and sleep up to 5 guests. Room service is available.

Continental or Filipino breakfast is available. Other amenities include a restaurant, a bar and a coffee shop. There's also a courtyard garden, an outdoor pool and a spa.

Address: EJ Blanco Drive Brgy. Piapi Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental 6200 Philippines

Website:https://sierrahotel.ph

Victoria's Haven

Directions

Victoria's Haven is a heritage inn nestled within a larger family, business property and is ideally situated in the municipality of Sibulan - within a ten minute ride from the airport or twenty from the pier. What began as a place of business eventually turned into a stylish and elegant residence surrounded by a manicured garden; a warm and welcoming home. This is a place where livelihoods were built and priceless childhood memories were made, a place of business and a home for life's best and simplest pleasures.

Address: National Highway Tubtubon Sibulan, Negros Oriental 6201 Philippines

Bravo Golf Hotel Resort and Spa

Directions

BRAVO HOTEL is a four-star hotel classification infrastructure project located just 10-minutes drive from the airport and marina. It is a delightful vacation to experience the best of Dumaguete and central Visayas.The estate features a golf course developed by Youichi Nagato, who has managed the well-known golf course in Japan. It also features a shooting range, where one may rent its equipment or bring your own equipment to do practice shooting.

Address: National Rd Sibulan Public Plaza, Negros Oriental 6201 Philippines

Rovira Suites

Directions

Rovira Suites is a contemporary boutique hotel at the heart of Dumaguete City with 30 all-suite rooms, each one designed with your comfort in mind. Rovira Suites has accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a garden. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The property provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

All rooms at the hotel come with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a bidet, slippers and a hairdryer. All rooms include an electric tea pot, while selected rooms also feature a kitchenette with a fridge and a minibar. At Rovira Suites the rooms include bed linen and towel. The accommodation offers a continental or à la carte breakfast.

Address: L. Rovira Rd, Bantayan Dumaguete, Negros Oriental 6200 Philippines

Website:https://rovirasuites.com

Facebook Page:https://www.facebook.com/RoviraSuitesDGT
