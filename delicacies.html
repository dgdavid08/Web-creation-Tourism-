Sans Rival Cakes and Pastries/ Sans Rival Bistro

Directions

Sans Rival Bistro, located along of the Rizal Boulevard, is one of the restaurants in Dumaguete which is facing the beautiful view of the ocean. A perfect place to spend some quality time with your friends and families while having a nice meal, or simply to enjoy a snack after a shopping trip through the city. Sans Rival Bistro is a sister company of the Sans Rival Cake House, which is famous for their signature dessert, the famous Silvanas. Nobody can resist this sweet delights and they are always welcome as a pasalubong (small gift).

Address: 3 San Jose Street, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental 6200 Philippines

Dagsa-an Restaurant, Dumaguete

Directions

Dagsaan Restaurant is a combination of cafeteria style and alar carte restaurant dedicated to Filipino cuisine and culture. Located at by the north boulevard of Dumaguete City (Northpoint) – hence its name from the root word “Dagsa” – the convergence of people and its local dishes. From ”Fish Tinula” (Filipino fish soup) to ”Balbacua” (meat stew) with classic local foods are available here and they are simply exquisite. Also, serve Lunch, and Dinner.

Address: N Blvd, Northpoint Dumaguete, Negros Oriental 6200 Philippines

Facebook Page:https://www.facebook.com/DagsaanRestaurant

Jo's Chicken Inato

Directions

Jo’s Chicken Inato restaurant has a cozy garden area with a very Filipino motif. While the garden’s furniture is made of bamboo, the tables inside are made of cross-sectional cuts from a narra tree. To be more pleasing to the senses, they serve their barbecued chicken in platters of banana leaf with rice and pickled of papaya or, “atchara.” People are encourage to used their hands when eating at this place ala “kamayan style”. The great location of this restaurant makes it easy to reach even in rush hours. Come to Jo's Chicken Inato - Sibulan to try perfectly cooked chicken. Spend a nice time here and share tasty halo halo with your friends.

Address: Silliman Avenue, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental 6200 Philippines

Hayahay: A Treehouse Bar and View Deck Restobar

Directions

One of the most well known party places in Dumaguete. It is a combination of Bar and Seafood restaurant on the boardwalk. This place has become one of the locals’ favorite dining spots because of its impressive location overlooking an ocean exquisite view.

Hayahay Treehouse Bar and View Deck remains distinctive among locals because of its native décor and ambiance. This open-air restaurant is a two-storey treehouse made of driftwood and nipa roof which gives off an inviting urban vibe and offers a cool dining experience.Hayahay Treehouse Bar & Viewdeck is one of the top rated place listed as Restaurant/cafe in Dumaguete City, Bar & Grill in Dumaguete City , Seafood Restaurant in Dumaguete City , Pizza Place in Dumaguete City.

Address: Flores Avenue, Dumaguete, Negros Oriental, 6200 Philippines
